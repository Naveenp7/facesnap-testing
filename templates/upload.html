Processing Information
What happens after upload?
  1. Photos are saved to the server
  2. Face detection identifies all faces in each photo
  3. Face recognition extracts unique features from each face
  4. Similar faces are grouped into clusters
  5. Photos are linked to their respective face clusters
Tips for best results
  • Upload clear, well-lit photos
  • Front-facing portraits work best
  • Group photos are fine - our system can detect multiple faces
  • Higher resolution photos yield better face detection
